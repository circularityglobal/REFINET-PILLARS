# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

REFInet is a technical community focused on building sovereign mesh infrastructure. We are committed to providing a welcoming and constructive environment for everyone.

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus on technical merit and collaborative problem-solving
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Prioritize the health of the community and the project
- Show empathy and kindness toward other community members

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Personal attacks, trolling, or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions or project activities
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any community channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@refinet.network**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy of the reporter.

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal from project spaces.
