How It Works

Six layers. Zero central authority.

7070REFInet Gopher
70Classic Gopher
7073GopherS / TLS
7074Proxy Gateway
7075WebSocket Bridge
1

Transport Layer

Dual-port Gopher server (7070 + 70) with optional TLS on 7073. All connections are plain TCP — fast, lightweight, and firewall-friendly.

2

Identity Layer

Ed25519 keypair generates your Pillar ID (PID). Every response is cryptographically signed. Peers verify content authenticity without a certificate authority.

3

Mesh Discovery

UDP multicast discovers local peers automatically. Bootstrap peers in peers.json connect you to the wider mesh. No DNS, no registrars.

4

Storage & Vault

SQLite WAL-mode database for registries. Encrypted local vault with ZKP authentication. Shamir Secret Sharing splits recovery keys across trusted parties.

5

EVM Gateway

Built-in RPC proxy to Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, and Base. SIWE wallet authentication links your on-chain identity to your PID.

6

Browser Bridge

WebSocket server on 7075 connects the browser extension to your Pillar. Browse Gopherspace, authenticate with SIWE, and manage your node from any browser.

What Is REFInet?

Six pillars of sovereign infrastructure.

Sovereign Mesh Node

No central server. No accounts. Your Pillar discovers peers via UDP multicast and replicates registries across the mesh autonomously.

Cryptographic Identity

Every Pillar has an Ed25519 keypair — your Pillar ID (PID). All content you serve is cryptographically signed and verifiable by anyone.

Web3 Native

Built-in EVM RPC gateway to 5 EVM-compatible chains. Connect your Ethereum wallet via SIWE. Run DApps from your own node. No middlemen.

Tor & Privacy

Optional Tor hidden service gives your node an .onion address. Combined with the encrypted vault and ZKP auth, you control your privacy completely.

Gopher Protocol

REFInet revives and extends the Gopher protocol — lightweight, fast, and impossible to censor. Your Pillar serves on TCP 7070.

Encrypted Vault

Local secret storage with zero-knowledge proof authentication and Shamir Secret Sharing for recovery. Your keys never leave your machine.
